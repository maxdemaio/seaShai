New Sea Shai

About

Located just a short distance from Newport's beaches, New Sea Shai has been serving award winning, authentic Japanese and Korean cuisine since 1987. Experience an exceptional selection of fresh sushi alongside our extensive menu of traditional Japanese and Korean dishes.
